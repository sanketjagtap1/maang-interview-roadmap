# Behavioral - Week 4

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Project Management / Delivery
- **Situation**: Tight deadline or scope change
- **Task**: Deliver quality solution on time
- **Action**: Prioritization, delegation, technical shortcuts (with trade-offs)
- **Result**: On-time delivery, stakeholder satisfaction, lessons learned

### Conflict Resolution
- **Situation**: Technical disagreement or team conflict
- **Task**: Resolve while maintaining relationships
- **Action**: Data-driven discussion, compromise, escalation if needed
- **Result**: Resolution, team cohesion, improved process

### Innovation / Problem Solving
- **Situation**: Unique technical challenge
- **Task**: Find creative solution
- **Action**: Research, experimentation, iteration
- **Result**: Solution effectiveness, adoption, impact

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Delivered under pressure
  - Disagreed with your manager/team
  - Solved a problem with limited resources
  - Improved a process or workflow
  - Handled a difficult stakeholder

## Preparation
- Finalize all STAR stories (8-10 total)
- Practice 90-second versions (interview format)
- Prepare "Why this company?" answer
- Prepare "Why leave current role?" (if applicable)
- Research recent company news/products
