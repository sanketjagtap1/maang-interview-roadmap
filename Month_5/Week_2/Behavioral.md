# Behavioral - Week 2

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Story Refinement
- **Polish**: Refine all stories for clarity
- **Practice**: Daily practice with timer
- **Feedback**: Get feedback from peers
- **Variation**: Prepare multiple versions

### Interview Readiness
- **Confidence**: Build interview confidence
- **Clarity**: Clear, concise communication
- **Examples**: Ready examples for all themes
- **Questions**: Thoughtful questions prepared

### Company Alignment
- **Research**: Deep research on target companies
- **Alignment**: Align stories with company values
- **Products**: Understand products/services
- **Culture**: Understand company culture

## Question Themes (Advanced)
- "Tell me about a failure" (with lessons learned)
- "How do you handle stress?"
- "Describe a conflict" (resolution focus)
- "What motivates you?"
- "Why are you leaving current role?"

## Preparation
- Practice all question types
- Prepare failure stories (with growth)
- Research company culture deeply
- Prepare thoughtful questions
- Mock interviews with feedback
