# Revision - Week 1

## What to Revise
- **Maintenance**: Keep skills sharp
- **Analysis**: Post-interview review
- **Improvement**: Continuous refinement
- **Confidence**: Maintain confidence

## Re-solve Strategy
1. **Light Practice**: Maintain skills
2. **Analysis**: Review each interview
3. **Improvement**: Refine based on feedback
4. **Confidence**: Maintain high confidence
5. **Readiness**: Stay interview-ready

## Focus Areas
- Execution: Strong interview performance
- Analysis: Learn from each interview
- Improvement: Continuous refinement
- Maintenance: Keep skills current

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Over-analyzing (paralysis)
- Neglecting maintenance
- Losing confidence
- Not learning from feedback

## Week 1 Assessment
- Interview performance tracking
- Feedback analysis
- Improvement identification
- Confidence maintenance
