# Behavioral - Week 1

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Final Refinement
- **Story Bank**: 12-15 polished stories
- **Practice**: Mock behavioral interviews
- **Variety**: Cover all question themes
- **Alignment**: Match to target companies

### Interview Skills
- **Communication**: Clear, structured, engaging
- **Examples**: Quantifiable, concrete results
- **Enthusiasm**: Show passion and energy
- **Questions**: Thoughtful questions ready

### Company Research
- **Values**: Deep understanding of company culture
- **Products**: Know products/services in detail
- **Tech Stack**: Research technologies and challenges
- **Recent News**: Stay updated on company news

## Question Themes (Practice)
- "Tell me about yourself" (2-min pitch)
- "Why this company?" (specific reasons)
- "Why this role?" (alignment with goals)
- "What are your weaknesses?" (growth-focused)
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

## Preparation
- Practice all stories with timer (90 sec, 2 min)
- Mock behavioral interviews
- Research all target companies deeply
- Prepare company-specific questions
- Review common interview mistakes
