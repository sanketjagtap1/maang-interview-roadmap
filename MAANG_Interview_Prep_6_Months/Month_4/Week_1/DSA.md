# DSA - Week 1

## Topics (Order)
1. Bit Manipulation
2. Math and Number Theory
3. Geometry Basics
4. Combinatorics Basics

## Patterns to Learn
- Bitwise operations (AND, OR, XOR, shifts)
- Power of 2, number of set bits
- Prime numbers, GCD, LCM
- Permutations and combinations

- Bit manipulation: XOR properties, bit masks
- Math: Fast exponentiation, binary search for sqrt

- Time complexity: O(1) for bit ops, O(log n) for math
