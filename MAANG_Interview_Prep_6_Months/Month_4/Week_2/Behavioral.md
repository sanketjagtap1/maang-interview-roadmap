# Behavioral - Week 2

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Code Review / Quality
- **Situation**: Code quality or review process challenge
- **Task**: Improve code quality or review process
- **Action**: Guidelines, automation, mentoring, examples
- **Result**: Bug reduction, faster onboarding, quality improvement

### Technical Leadership
- **Situation**: Leading technical initiative or team
- **Task**: Drive technical direction, mentor team
- **Action**: Architecture decisions, knowledge sharing, mentoring
- **Result**: Team growth, technical excellence, project success

### Process Improvement
- **Situation**: Inefficient development or deployment process
- **Task**: Improve team efficiency or quality
- **Action**: Process analysis, tool selection, automation
- **Result**: Time saved, quality improved, team satisfaction

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Improved code review process
  - Mentored a junior developer
  - Led a technical initiative
  - Improved development workflow
  - Established technical standards

## Preparation
- Add leadership/mentoring stories
- Practice explaining technical concepts simply
- Prepare examples of process improvements
- Research company's engineering culture
