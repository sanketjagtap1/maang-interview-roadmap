# System Design - Week 3

## Core Concepts
- Company-specific system design (Google, Amazon, Meta, etc.)
- Domain-specific designs (e-commerce, social media, etc.)
- Design trade-offs and decision making
- Design presentation and communication

## Interview Focus Points
- **Company focus**: Research target company's systems
- **Domain expertise**: E-commerce, social media, streaming, etc.
- **Trade-offs**: Explain decisions clearly
- **Communication**: Present design clearly, handle questions

## Practice
- Design systems specific to target companies
- Practice explaining trade-offs
- Mock system design interviews
- Review real-world system designs

## Reading
- Company engineering blogs
- Real-world system design case studies
- Domain-specific design patterns
- System design interview tips
