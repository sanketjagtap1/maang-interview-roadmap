# Behavioral - Week 1

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### System Design / Architecture
- **Situation**: Large-scale system design or redesign project
- **Task**: Design scalable, reliable architecture
- **Action**: Requirements gathering, trade-off analysis, architecture decisions
- **Result**: System scalability, reliability metrics, cost optimization

### Performance at Scale
- **Situation**: System performance issues at scale
- **Task**: Optimize for millions of users/requests
- **Action**: Profiling, bottleneck identification, optimization strategies
- **Result**: Latency reduction, throughput increase, cost savings

### Technical Decision Making
- **Situation**: Critical technical decision with multiple options
- **Task**: Choose best solution considering trade-offs
- **Action**: Research, POC, cost-benefit analysis, team discussion
- **Result**: Successful implementation, lessons learned

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Designed a system handling millions of requests
  - Optimized a system for scale
  - Made a critical architecture decision
  - Handled a system migration
  - Worked on a high-traffic system

## Preparation
- Refine system design stories with scale numbers
- Practice explaining architecture decisions
- Prepare examples of trade-offs (consistency vs availability)
- Research company's scale and challenges
