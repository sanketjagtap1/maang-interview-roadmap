# Behavioral - Week 2

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### System Design / Architecture
- **Situation**: System performance issue or scalability challenge
- **Task**: Redesign or optimize existing system
- **Action**: Architecture decisions, trade-offs considered, migration plan
- **Result**: Performance metrics, cost reduction, improved reliability

### Failure / Recovery
- **Situation**: Production incident or system failure
- **Task**: Minimize downtime, restore service
- **Action**: Debugging process, rollback strategy, communication
- **Result**: Recovery time, lessons learned, preventive measures

### Innovation / Initiative
- **Situation**: Identified improvement opportunity
- **Task**: Propose and implement solution
- **Action**: Research, prototyping, stakeholder buy-in
- **Result**: Adoption rate, efficiency gains, business impact

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Designed a system from scratch
  - Handled a production outage
  - Optimized a slow system
  - Made a technical decision with incomplete information
  - Mentored a junior developer

## Preparation
- Refine Week 1 stories based on feedback
- Add 2-3 new stories (system design focus)
- Practice technical depth without jargon overload
- Prepare questions to ask interviewer
