# Behavioral - Week 1

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Leadership/Impact
- **Situation**: Describe a critical production issue or feature deadline
- **Task**: Your responsibility and constraints
- **Action**: Technical decisions, team coordination, problem-solving steps
- **Result**: Quantifiable impact (uptime, revenue, user satisfaction)

### Technical Challenge
- **Situation**: Complex technical problem (scalability, performance, architecture)
- **Task**: Requirements and constraints
- **Action**: Research, design decisions, implementation approach
- **Result**: Metrics (latency reduction, cost savings, scalability achieved)

### Collaboration
- **Situation**: Cross-functional project or conflict
- **Task**: Deliverable and stakeholders
- **Action**: Communication strategy, compromise, technical solutions
- **Result**: Project success, relationship outcomes

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Dealt with a difficult technical problem
  - Disagreed with a technical decision
  - Had to learn something new quickly
  - Improved system performance
  - Worked with a difficult team member

## Preparation
- Write 5-7 STAR stories (2-3 min each)
- Quantify results (numbers, percentages, time saved)
- Practice concise delivery (avoid rambling)
- Prepare follow-up answers for "What would you do differently?"
