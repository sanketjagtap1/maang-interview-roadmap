# DSA - Week 1

## Topics (Order)
1. Interview Execution
2. Post-Interview Analysis
3. Continuous Improvement
4. Skill Maintenance

## Focus
- **Interviews**: Active interview period
- **Analysis**: Post-interview review
- **Improvement**: Continuous refinement
- **Maintenance**: Keep skills sharp

## Practice Strategy

### During Interviews
- Light daily practice (1 problem)
- Maintain problem-solving skills
- Review patterns as needed
- Stay sharp between interviews

### Post-Interview
- Analyze interview performance
- Identify areas for improvement
- Review mistakes and learnings
- Adjust preparation strategy

### Continuous Learning
- Learn from each interview
- Refine problem-solving approach
- Improve communication skills
- Build on feedback

## Focus
- Execution: Perform well in interviews
- Analysis: Learn from each interview
- Improvement: Continuous refinement
- Maintenance: Keep skills current
