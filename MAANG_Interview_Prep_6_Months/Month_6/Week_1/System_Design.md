# System Design - Week 1

## Core Concepts
- Interview Execution
- Design Presentation
- Post-Interview Analysis
- Continuous Improvement

## Interview Focus Points
- **Execution**: Perform well in design interviews
- **Presentation**: Clear, structured design presentation
- **Analysis**: Post-interview review and learning
- **Improvement**: Continuous refinement

## Practice Strategy

### During Interviews
- Light design review
- Maintain design thinking
- Review key concepts
- Stay confident

### Post-Interview
- Analyze design performance
- Review feedback
- Identify improvement areas
- Refine design approach

### Continuous Learning
- Learn from each interview
- Refine design skills
- Improve communication
- Build on feedback

## Focus
- Execution: Strong design interviews
- Analysis: Learn from experience
- Improvement: Refine continuously
- Confidence: Maintain high confidence
