# Behavioral - Week 3

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Architecture / Design
- **Situation**: System redesign or new architecture needed
- **Task**: Design scalable, maintainable solution
- **Action**: Requirements analysis, trade-offs, prototyping, documentation
- **Result**: Improved scalability, reduced complexity, team adoption

### Testing / Quality Assurance
- **Situation**: Quality issues or need for better testing strategy
- **Task**: Improve test coverage or quality processes
- **Action**: Test strategy, automation, CI/CD integration
- **Result**: Bug reduction, faster releases, confidence in deployments

### Stakeholder Management
- **Situation**: Managing expectations with product/business stakeholders
- **Task**: Deliver on time while maintaining quality
- **Action**: Communication, scope management, technical trade-offs
- **Result**: Successful delivery, stakeholder trust, relationship building

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Designed a system architecture
  - Improved testing or quality processes
  - Had to say no to a feature request
  - Dealt with scope creep
  - Made a technical decision that affected the business

## Preparation
- Add architecture/design stories
- Prepare examples of technical trade-offs
- Practice explaining complex systems simply
- Review company's engineering blog/articles
