# Behavioral - Week 1

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Technical Leadership
- **Situation**: Leading a technical initiative or architecture decision
- **Task**: Drive technical direction, get team alignment
- **Action**: Research, proof of concept, documentation, team discussions
- **Result**: Adoption, improved system quality, team growth

### Code Review / Quality
- **Situation**: Maintaining code quality at scale
- **Task**: Establish or improve code review process
- **Action**: Guidelines, automation, mentoring, examples
- **Result**: Bug reduction, faster onboarding, consistent patterns

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **Situation**: Working with product, design, or other teams
- **Task**: Deliver feature with multiple stakeholders
- **Action**: Communication, requirement clarification, technical feasibility
- **Result**: Successful delivery, stakeholder satisfaction, relationship building

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Led a technical project
  - Improved code quality standards
  - Worked with non-technical stakeholders
  - Made a technical decision that was later proven wrong
  - Mentored someone technically

## Preparation
- Refine existing stories with technical depth
- Add leadership-focused stories
- Practice explaining technical concepts simply
- Prepare follow-up questions about team culture
