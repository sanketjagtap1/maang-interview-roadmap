# Behavioral - Week 3

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Company-Specific Preparation
- **Research**: Target company's values, culture, products
- **Align Stories**: Match stories to company values
- **Product Knowledge**: Understand company's products/services
- **Technical Focus**: Research company's tech stack, challenges

### Interview Readiness
- **Story Refinement**: Finalize all STAR stories (12-15 total)
- **Practice**: 90-second versions of each story
- **Questions**: Prepare questions to ask interviewers
- **Weaknesses**: Prepare "weakness" answer (growth-focused)

### Communication
- **Clarity**: Practice explaining complex concepts simply
- **Examples**: Have concrete examples ready
- **Enthusiasm**: Show passion for technology and company
- **Follow-up**: Prepare for follow-up questions

## Question Themes
- "Why this company?"
- "Why this role?"
- "Tell me about yourself"
- "What are your weaknesses?"
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"

## Preparation
- Research target companies thoroughly
- Align stories with company values
- Practice all common behavioral questions
- Prepare thoughtful questions for interviewers
