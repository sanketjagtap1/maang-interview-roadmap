# Behavioral - Week 2

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Performance Optimization
- **Situation**: System performance bottleneck or scalability issue
- **Task**: Identify and fix performance problems
- **Action**: Profiling, bottleneck identification, optimization strategies
- **Result**: Latency reduction, throughput increase, cost savings

### Technical Debt Management
- **Situation**: Accumulated technical debt affecting velocity
- **Task**: Balance new features with refactoring
- **Action**: Prioritization, incremental refactoring, team buy-in
- **Result**: Improved velocity, reduced bugs, better maintainability

### Innovation / Experimentation
- **Situation**: Opportunity to try new technology or approach
- **Task**: Evaluate and implement new solution
- **Action**: Research, POC, risk assessment, gradual rollout
- **Result**: Improved system, team learning, adoption success

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Optimized a slow system
  - Managed technical debt
  - Tried a new technology that failed
  - Made a mistake in production
  - Had to deprecate a system

## Preparation
- Add performance/optimization stories
- Prepare failure stories (with lessons learned)
- Practice explaining trade-offs clearly
- Research company's tech stack, align stories
