# Behavioral - Week 4

## STAR Stories to Prepare

### Problem Solving / Debugging
- **Situation**: Complex production bug or system issue
- **Task**: Debug and fix under pressure
- **Action**: Systematic debugging, log analysis, hypothesis testing
- **Result**: Resolution time, system stability, process improvements

### Process Improvement
- **Situation**: Inefficient process or workflow
- **Task**: Improve team efficiency or quality
- **Action**: Identify bottlenecks, propose solutions, implement changes
- **Result**: Time saved, quality improved, team adoption

### Handling Ambiguity
- **Situation**: Unclear requirements or changing scope
- **Task**: Deliver solution with incomplete information
- **Action**: Ask clarifying questions, make assumptions, iterate
- **Result**: Successful delivery, stakeholder satisfaction

## Question Themes
- "Tell me about a time you..."
  - Debugged a difficult production issue
  - Improved a process or workflow
  - Worked with ambiguous requirements
  - Had to learn something quickly
  - Dealt with a difficult customer/stakeholder

## Preparation
- Finalize all STAR stories (10-12 total)
- Practice 2-min versions (interview format)
- Prepare "Why this role?" answer
- Prepare questions about team structure, growth opportunities
- Research interviewer's background (if known)
